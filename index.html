Highlights

Browse Conveniently

With Bridgeport, you can engage with twitter in a more convenient, more accessible place. Be part of the conversation without having to open your browser.

Shockingly New, yet Timelessly Mac

Design inspired by the best of Google and the best of Apple, fused together to create a responsive fluid user interface that's beautiful enough to not get in the way.

The New Native

Bridgeport is developed in GitHub's Electron for better compatibility with progressive web apps, and a more web oriented future. HTML Builds it, CSS makes it pretty, and JavaScript makes it work.

$FR.EE

Clients for a free service shouldn't cost money, so Bridgeport doesn't. It's free with no non-twitter ads. That means no interruptions, no annoying pop-ups, and no bullshit. Just you and whatever you get up to when no one's watching. We don't judge.
